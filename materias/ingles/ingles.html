Regular and Irregular verbs

Verbs are words or group of words that express an action (such as eat) an event (such as happen) or a state (such as exist).

The infinitive of the verbs in english language is formed with preposition to or without it: to understand, to eat, to speak, to work, to cry

The verbs in English language ca be regular and irregular:

Prepositions of Place

The positons of place are used to indicate the position of a person, object and a place. The prepositions of place are:

  • Near: perto de, da, do.
  • Above: acima de, da, do.
  • Behind: atrásde, da, do.
  • Beside: ao ladode, da, do.
  • On: em cima de, da, do.
  • In: dentro de, da, do.
  • Out of: fora de, da, do.
  • Outside: fora
  • Around: em volta de, da, do.
  • Under: em baixo de, da, do.
  • Inside: dentro de, da, do.
  • Between: entre(indica posição no meio de 2 pessoas ou coisas)
  • Among:entre(indica posição no meio de 3 ou mais pessoas ou coisas) At: em, no, na (indica uma posição especifica, endereço)
  • Up: no topo, no cimo.
  • In front: na frente de, da, do.
  • Next to: proximo de, da, do.

Example: The clack is above the table

The teacher is in front of the students

I am next to your house

The bag is behind the door.

Prepositions of Direction

The prepositions of direction are used to indicate a location of a person, object and place. The prepositions of direction are:

  • To:em direcção de, da, do, para, à, até, a fim de, em, com, de, da.

    Example: I am going to school

  • Into: dentro, para dentro(in-dentro / to-para) {indica movimento dentro de um espaço, objecto e volme}

    Example: Mary jumped into the water
    Don’t run into the house

  • Toward: em direcção a, de, da, do.

    Example: There is a strange man walking toward you

  • Through: através de (passando por dentro)

    Example: I saw the dog trough the window

Other prepositions of direction are:

  • Left:esquerda
  • Right:direita
  • To turn:virar
  • Turn left:viraaesquerda
  • Turn right:vira a direita
  • There: ali, lá
  • Straight: em frente

Active and Passive voice

Active voice: can be used in present and past.

Pronouns used in active voice

  • I - Eu
  • You - Tu
  • He - Ele
  • She - Ela
  • It - Ele ou ela para objectos e animais
  • We - Nós
  • You - Vós
  • They - Eles

Passive voice: is used to high light the actions

Pronouns used in passive voice

  • Me - eu
  • You - Tu
  • Him - Ele
  • Her - Ela
  • Its - Ele ou ela objectos e animais
  • Us - Nós
  • You - Vós
  • Them - Elas

EXAMPLE: They play football - active voice / football is played by them - passive voice
